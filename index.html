We Are UNYA Aalborg

A diverse student-run, non-profit organization seeking to educate young people about the purpose of the United Nations and the importance of human rights, as well as promote and encourage UN values and activities among Danish youth, society and the international community of Aalborg.

What we do

Empower young people to get involved in international affairs and civil society

Inspire

UNYA Aalborg aims to inspire individual change and growth in youth

Thank you for showing interest in joining UNYA.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What does UNYA stand for?

UNYA stands for United Nations Youth Asssociation.

Who can become a UNYA Volunteer?

Since we are a youth association, our volunteers are between the ages of 18 and 35. Anyone can become a UNYA volunteer who is internationally-minded, motivated to help us educate, empower and inspire the youth of Denmark, and has some spare hours every month to attend our meeting and possibly events. Nevertheless, to make sure that our team is as well put together and as driven as possible, you do need to go through a short screening process to become part of our team.

How is UNYA related to the United Nations?

UNYA DK is a daughter organization of the United Nations Association of Denmark (UNA DK), which is part of the World Federation of United Nations Associations (WFUNA). Additionally, UNYA DK is part of the United Nations Youth Associations Network (UNYANET).

Who can I contact if I have a question that isn't answered here?

Please write to: inquiries@unya-aalborg.dk for any further questions you may have.
